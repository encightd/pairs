About Us

Welcome to Pan-African Research. Pan-African Investment & Research Services (PAIRS) has demonstrated a track record of research excellence since 2005. The company’s team comprises complementary competencies in economics and financial analysis. PAIRS is the African associate of Global Source Partners, a New York-based economic research advisory firm, specializing in the emerging market macroeconomic research covering the Americas, Asia and Europe. The in-house team is able to draw on the vast experience and skills of growing network of professional associates in Southern Africa and globally. The company’s client base includes both listed and unlisted companies in South Africa and internationally.
